QUARTZITE

Quartzite is a natural stone formed from sandstone that has been metamorphosed under heat and pressure. It is known for its durability, strength, and beautiful veining. Quartzite is perfect for high-traffic areas like kitchens and bathrooms, including countertops, backsplashes, and even flooring, as it is resistant to heat, scratches, and stains.

MARBLE

Marble is a natural stone known for its elegance and unique veining patterns. It is softer and more porous than other stones, which requires more maintenance. Marble is ideal for bathroom vanities, countertops, and backsplashes, offering a luxurious look, but it should be used with care in high-traffic kitchen areas due to its susceptibility to scratches and stains.

GRANITE

Granite is a durable, natural stone made from cooled magma, known for its strength, variety of colors, and resistance to heat and scratches. It is an excellent choice for kitchen countertops, bathroom vanities, and backsplashes due to its longevity and low maintenance requirements.

DOLOMITE

Dolomite is a natural stone similar to marble but more durable, known for its smooth texture and subtle veining. It is resistant to heat and scratches, making it a great option for kitchen countertops, bathroom vanities, and backsplashes. However, it is still somewhat porous, so sealing is recommended for long-term maintenance.
